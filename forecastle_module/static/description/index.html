Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Odoo propose the notion of calendar to organize your day but do not propose a full meeting app.

CLuedoo behavior : You can fully manage your meeting:

Before: create draft - submit & invite people - send reminder - define agenda & target time.

During: get the watch & the live cost of your meeting based on the participants.

After: close your meeting with accrual hours (based on the watch or manually filled), make the MOM (internal & external MOM) then print it or send it by email and close the meeting.

Module + : You can connect this app with timesheet to create timesheet activities based on the meeting info.Business case

Business case : So, every tool to automatically fill timesheet is a plus for the company. and here, the meeting responsible is filling the timesheet for all the attendees!

CLuedoo recommendation : We recommend to install the meeting app on 100% of the database.

According your organization, you can define your internal process - just manage After meeting and MOM or make a full meeting management.
